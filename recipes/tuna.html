Tuna Roll

Home

Description

Tuna roll is a sushi roll of just tuna. It is very good if the tuna is a little fatty

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Raw tuna
  • Nori sheets
  • Avocado slices

Steps

  1. Cut avocado into strips
  2. Place nori sheet with shiny side down
  3. Use a spoon to spread an even layer of rice on top of the nori
  4. Flip the nori over
  5. Add the filling to the end closest to you
  6. Roll the sheet over the ingredients
  7. Cut into desired pieces
