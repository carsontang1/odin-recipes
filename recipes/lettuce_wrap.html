Asian Lettuce Wraps

Description: Some kind of meat wrapped in lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 16 Lettuce Leaves
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp minced picked ginger
  • 1 dash Asian chile pepper sauce
  • 8 oz. can water chestnuts
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 2 tsp Asian sesame oil
  1. Rince lettuce leaves and pat dry
  2. Heat oil in skillet, add beef and cook until browned and crumbly. Drain and discard grease. Transfer beef to a bowl. Add onion to the same skillet and cook until tender
  3. Stir in sauces and stuff.
  4. Arrange ingredients like picture
